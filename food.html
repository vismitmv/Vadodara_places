Canera Coffee House

 

The place is more than 50 years old and situated in the center of Baroda city. Opens up at 07 in the morning - they serve specific breakfast early morning - only Poha, Upma or Sheera (rarely). After 8 they start full fledged kitchen with all south indian items and Poona misal and rest all. Poona misal is one if the hot selling items here - it is not typical maharashtrian misal but Gujju and south version of it. You will find bit sweet but tasty, it has poha, sprouted moong, sev, onion and dahi, some rasaa (not theekha) as well.
